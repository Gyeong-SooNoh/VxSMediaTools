*** DXT STIP Sender ***
1) Contents
- Capture HD video frames from a Decklink capture card
- Encode the captured frame with DXT 
- Generates STIP packets (STIP initiation packet and transport packet)
- Packetize the DXT compressed frame as STIP transport packets and transmit it to a destination host
